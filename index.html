Get Quote

Click Get Quote, we will contact you with an estimated repair cost of your device. After you've received a quote from us and would like to move forward with the repair process, the next step is to mail-in your device.

Mail-In Process

When preparing your device for mail-in, it is "very important" that you apply
"2-3 inches of protection" on all sides of your device to insure that your device "does not sustain any damage" during transit.

Boxed up and ready to ship?

Click Mail-In & fill out the form only if you will be shipping your device the same day or within "1 business day" of "mail-in form" completion.

Payment Process

A $10 USD diagnostic fee is due the day of shipment via "CashApp" .
Repair costs may be paid after a successful repair via "CashApp" .

(We Do-Not Accept Any Other Form Of Payment Method).
After receipt of payment, your device will be shipped back to you immediately with tracking.

Questions and Answers.

How much is shipping costs?

We do not cover the cost of out going or return shipping. Before shipping your device be sure to have 2 inches of protection on all sides. It is important to add this protection in the event of possible damage, the 2 inches of protection will insure there will be no damage during shipping.

Where do I send my device to?

We accept mail-ins at our home base location, but only after you have contacted us first. The address is as follows.

ASAP PC FIX
PO BOX 12091
Winston-Salem , NC 27117

How much does a repair cost?

You can click around the site to find prices for various issues from Mac laptop repair to data recovery. If you have a specific issue not addressed here, feel free to call us, email, or live chat. Knowing the model number and year of your machine is helpful to give you a price.

How long does a repair take?

This really depends on what you are having done. Most screen repairs are done the same day. Same for keyboards, track pads. Data recovery can be anywhere from one day to a month, motherboard repair can take anywhere from an hour to six weeks depending on the severity of the issue. For your particular situation, feel free to email or call us to inquire about the turnaround time for your particular device.

Diagnostic cost?

Yes. Diagnostic fee is required, the fee covers preliminary testing for the full functionality of your device before the diagnostic procedure begins. Depending on the issue can take an hour or more to get to the root of the problem.

Is there anything else I need to know?

Yes, before you send a repair, contact us first
